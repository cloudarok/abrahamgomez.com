i’m an entrepreneur, investor, and a lover of technology.


<span id="b"><b>my loves</b></span>
family, friendship, music, travel, and art.


<span id="b"><b>early days</b></span>
my journey began with chrono trigger, final fantasy, and ragnarok online. a quick shoutout to renegade from command & conquer. i was once ranked #2 in the world.

my love for all things tech started there, with computers and video games. ragnarok, the mmorpg, taught me teamwork, communication, and economics. for my nephews reading this, it also taught me delegation. i had them grinding for ore and rare items. i’m not proud of it.

chrono trigger introduced me to art, music, and storytelling. (please listen to “dreams of a shore near another world” from chrono cross.) renegade taught me about hard work and the grit required to become the best, even if i never reached #1. feel free to ask me why.


in truth, my journey really began thanks to my parents. they have given me everything and continue to do so. they sacrificed their freedom to support and nurture me and my siblings.

to adolfo gomez and maria gomez, i am forever grateful. i love you.


<span id="b"><b>my work</b></span>
<li>2009: i graduated high school. i thought i had made it and decided to follow an unconventional path. my life has been that ever since. cue: "<span id="c"><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ql9-82oV2JE" target="_blank">you’re going to go far, kid</a></span>" and "<span id="c"><a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dvgZkm1xWPE" target="_blank">viva la vida</a></span>"</li>

<li>2012: i became ceo of godfruits, a christian publication that reached millions of monthly impressions. i learned about the internet, distribution, and social media.</li>

<li>2013: i discovered dogecoin and began mining it on day two.</li>

<li>2014: i co-founded viralcharge with friends in colorado springs, an inspirational content platform that quickly became a top 1,000 website. i learned what it meant to be a bad boss.</li>

<li>2016: i became a partner and cmo at fishermen labs, an incredible design and development agency. i learned from people who were smarter, more disciplined, and more hard-working than me.</li>

<li>2017: i co-founded wild & west with my brothers quintin, cole, derek, and angel. we focused on digital marketing for e-commerce and non-profits. i learned what it meant to struggle.</li>

<li>2022: i began advising <span><a href="https://www.ahmeddalmook.com/" target="_blank">hh sheikh ahmed dalmook al maktoum</a></span> on venture and web3 investments. i learned about humility, power, and leadership.</li>

<li>2025: i started ragnarok, an all-weather investment platform designed to operate across venture, growth, and public markets, focused on ownership, alignment, and long-term value creation.</li>

<li>2026: scaling ragnarok.com into a global platform and continuing the pursuit of freedom</li>
